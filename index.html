01. BIOGRAPHY
GXSOUL is a Nepalese hip hop group founded in 2006. "GXSOUL" is an abbreviation for "Gorkhali Extreme Soul". The group's founder members are Bibas Giri and Nima Pun, also known as viber BI / Bibaz Jung Giri and Nimasang respectively. Bulletflo, also known as Suraj Bikram Thapa, was included in the group from 2011 to 2016.
02.
CAREER
GXSOUL's most successful songs include "Raat Vari", "Satayera", "Mitho Sapana" and "Yo Timlay Garda Ho", acclaimed as one of the most viewed Nepali hip hop videos on YouTube. In 2014 their collaboration with COD (Crews On Destiny) 'Maridinchu' and Ankit Dhakal 'Ek Muskaan' topped local charts and went popular among their national and international fan base. GXSOUL have performed various stage shows in Nepal, Bhutan, India, Hong Kong, United Arab Emirates, Australia and the United Kingdom.
